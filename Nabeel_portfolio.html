Case Study

Cookie House: 30 Day Content Strategy

Instagram Content Strategy

Academic Project
Duration 30 Days
Framework Hook / Body / CTA
Designed a full 30 day Instagram content calendar for Cookie House using the Hook, Body, CTA structure: every post engineered to stop the scroll, deliver value, and drive one clear action.
Hook
First 1 to 2 seconds designed to interrupt the scroll, a bold visual or line that creates curiosity or relatability.
Body
The value delivery: product story, behind the scenes, or a reason to care, kept tight and visual first for Instagram's format.
CTA
One clear next action per post: comment, save, DM, or visit, never more than one ask.
